Business Intelligence

The BI world describes a set of strategies, technologies, tools, practices, and apps that companies use to acquire, analyze, model, and visualize data in order to generate valuable information for the organization. In this sense, digital services based on artificial intelligence are essential to produce forward-looking reports and make tactical decisions that allow institutions to get ahead of the competition.

Organizational Development

It is a set of strategies that an organization implements with the aim of improving its operations and boosting its growth in the market. This approach implies prioritizing the human element and productivity in a sustainable framework. Specifically, it is about identifying, evaluating and transforming the factors that benefit or harm the organizational structure of a company.
