Do you want to know about machine learning and deep learning?

ML & DL

Within the branches of AI development, the terms are usually included: machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DP). Both work from the semi-autonomous combination of heterogeneous elements coming from multiple structured and unstructured databases.

In general, ML models are divided into supervised (to solve classification and regression issues), unsupervised models (to deal with clustering and dimension reduction problems, among others), and reinforcement learning models (related to automobiles, live translations, skills learning by robots, etc.).

Deep learning (DL) involves learning processes generated from neural networks and convulsion layers. Although this type of learning has great predictive potential (especially with images), its main disadvantage lies in the impossibility of knowing the step by step of conducted by algorithms, which, in turn, it leads us into an ethical problem.
